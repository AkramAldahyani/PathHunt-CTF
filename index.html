02 // Workflow

The hunt, in three moves.

Most CTF pages hide two things: a flag, and a hint to the next page. Your job is to find both.

→
Land on a page
Read every visible word. Then open DevTools and read everything you can't see.
⌖
Find the flag
Comments, cookies, responses, scripts, styles — the flag is there. The challenge is where.
✓
Follow the trail
Each flag comes with a path to the next challenge. Submit the flag. Move on. Repeat.
